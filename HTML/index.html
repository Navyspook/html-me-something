Recruit Training Command (RTC)

Great Lakes, IL

1996

My navy joureny started with boot camp in Great Lake, IL. I was there from June through August. The summer's are hot!

Naval Technical Training Center (NTTC) Corry Station and Fort Huachuca

Pensacola, FL and Sierra Vista, AZ

1996 - 1997

My first stop out of boot camp was NTTC in Pensacola. I attended Cryptologic Technician (Collections) "A" school and Direct Support (DIRSUP) "C" School. The white sand beaches were great for decompression time.

The next stop was Morse Code school in Fort Huachuca. Months of dits and dahs. Tomb Stone was a short ride away and was neat to see.

Naval Security Group Activity (NSGA) Northwest

Chesapeake, VA

1998 - 2000

The end of my first chapter in the Navy was at NSGA Northwest. I was DIRSUP and rode different ships from the east coast of the US. I was honorably discharged from the US Navy in June of 2000, 4 years into my journey.

USS Mobile Bay (CG-53)

San Diego, CA

2002 - 2005

I reenlisted in the Navy in January 2002 aftera brief stint in the Army National Guard and a couple semesters at WVU Parkersburg. The USS Mobile Bay was a great command. Did six and nine month deployments in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom and Operation Enduring Freedom.
